## SLIDE 2: The Problem

### Industries Are Stuck in Disconnected Workflows

**Drug Discovery**: 10-15 years, $2.6B per approved drug
- AI predicts molecules, but doesn't test them
- Robots run experiments, but don't learn
- **Result**: Disconnected, slow, expensive

**Manufacturing**: 6-12 weeks from design to part
- CAD software designs, but can't manufacture
- 3D printers build, but don't optimize
- **Result**: Long iteration cycles, high failure rates

**Healthcare**: Reactive medicine treats symptoms after they appear
- Wearables collect data, but don't predict
- Doctors review dashboards, but act too late
- **Result**: 80% of chronic disease complications are preventable but not prevented

### The Pattern

All share:
- Long iteration cycles (months to get feedback)
- Expensive failures (wasted time/money)
- Disconnected systems (design ≠ execution ≠ analysis)
- Incumbents can't integrate (software-only OR hardware-only)
